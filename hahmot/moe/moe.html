Moammar Morris "Moe" Szyslak

Moammar Morris "Moe" Szyslak (pronounced /ˈsɪzlæk/ or "siz-lack"), also known as Moe the Bartender, is the proprietor and bartender of "Moe's Tavern", a Springfield bar frequented by Homer and his friends. He has once stated that he moved to Springfield from the Netherlands. He also owns Moe's Bagels in Colorado and Moe's Express in the Food Court area of the Springfield Mall.
