Homer Jay Simpson

Homer is characterized by his immense apathy towards work, overweight weight, love for food, and short attention span. He frequently spends his evenings drinking Duff Beer at Moe's Tavern and, as depicted in the episode "Duffless" (season four, 1993), has exhibited signs of full-blown alcoholism.
